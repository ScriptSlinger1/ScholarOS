The ScholarOS Mission

From your first assignment
to your acceptance letter.

The path to your dream university is complicated. We built the operating system that simplifies the journey, helping you master your GPA and conquer your applications.

Stay Organized

Stop juggling syllabus PDFs and loose notes. We unify your deadlines, exams, and extracurriculars into one intelligent dashboard that adapts to your life.

Study Efficiently

Work smarter, not harder. Our AI transforms passive reading into active recall questions and concise summaries, helping you retain more information in less time.

Get Admitted

Bridge the gap between high school and higher ed. Use our application tracker and AI essay grader to perfect your personal statement and stand out to admissions officers.

We know the pressure.

Being a student today is harder than ever. You are expected to maintain a perfect GPA, lead extracurricular clubs, volunteer, and somehow find time to write compelling essays for college applications.

We built ScholarOS because we were tired of using five different apps just to get through a single homework assignment.

We wanted a tool that didn't just list our tasks, but actually helped us complete them and leverage them for our future.
