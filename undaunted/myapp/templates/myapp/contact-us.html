Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked support queries.

Is there a student discount for ScholarOS Pro?

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email address receive 50% off all annual plans. You can apply this during the checkout process within the app.

Can I integrate ScholarOS with Canvas or Blackboard?

Absolutely. ScholarOS supports full LMS integration. Simply go to Settings > Integrations to sync your assignments and deadlines automatically.
